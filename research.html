Research Overview

My research focuses on advanced manufacturing and materials processing, with emphasis on microstructure–property relationships in high-temperature alloys and metal matrix composites. I integrate powder metallurgy, additive manufacturing, thermomechanical processing, and advanced characterization techniques to design high-performance structural materials.

1. Inconel 718 Composite Development

Development of graphene nanoplatelet (GNP) and carbon nanotube (CNT) reinforced Inconel 718 composites using Spark Plasma Sintering (SPS) and Selective Laser Melting (SLM).

  • Optimization of ball milling parameters for uniform reinforcement dispersion
  • Microstructural refinement and strengthening mechanism analysis
  • Mechanical testing: tensile, hardness, compression
  • Tribological and corrosion performance evaluation
  • FIB-TEM and EBSD analysis for interfacial characterization

2. Additive Manufacturing of High-Performance Alloys

Processing-structure-property investigations of metallic alloys fabricated using additive manufacturing techniques.

  • Selective Laser Melting (SLM) of nanocomposite powders
  • Binder Jetting of tool steels
  • Laser parameter optimization and melt pool control
  • Densification and defect mitigation strategies

3. Surface Engineering & High-Temperature Performance

Evaluation of protective coatings and surface treatments for reducing high-temperature oxidation and decarburization in structural steels.

  • Electrochemical Impedance Spectroscopy (EIS)
  • Potentiodynamic polarization testing
  • High-temperature oxidation resistance
  • Industrial collaborations with forging partners

4. Characterization & Analytical Tools

  • Scanning Electron Microscopy (SEM/EDS)
  • X-Ray Diffraction (XRD)
  • Electron Backscatter Diffraction (EBSD)
  • Image-based defect quantification
  • Statistical modeling and optimization (MATLAB, Minitab)

Current Direction

Ongoing work focuses on understanding nanoscale reinforcement–matrix interactions in SPS-processed superalloy composites and advancing additive manufacturing routes for improved strength-ductility balance in high-temperature structural materials.
