Teaching Philosophy

My teaching approach integrates theoretical understanding with hands-on application. I emphasize safety, process awareness, and engineering judgment while mentoring students to connect design, manufacturing, and material performance. By combining laboratory instruction with real-world industrial examples, I aim to prepare students for both research and manufacturing environments.
